Basement Waterproofing for Bath Homeowners

Bath is a historic village in Loyalist Township, situated between Napanee and Kingston along the north shore of Lake Ontario. Its location close to the lake creates elevated groundwater tables throughout much of the community, making basement waterproofing particularly important for Bath area homeowners.

Many homes in Bath and Loyalist Township were built in the 1970s and 1980s, when original waterproofing membranes, drain tile, and parging were applied to newly poured foundations. Decades of freeze-thaw cycling, root intrusion, and soil settlement have left many of these original systems at or past their service life — and water is finding new ways in.

Common waterproofing issues in Bath include spring flooding pressure from Lake Ontario's proximity raising the water table, wall seepage through concrete block or poured foundations, floor-wall joint leaks under hydrostatic pressure from below, foundation cracks caused by settlement or freeze-thaw cycling, and deteriorated weeping tile that no longer effectively directs groundwater away from the foundation.

AW Waterproofing Solutions serves Bath and all of Loyalist Township with the same expertise and certified Duraflex installation capabilities that protect homes throughout our Napanee base area. We bring the full range of waterproofing solutions to every Bath project.

Our Waterproofing Services in Bath

  • Interior Waterproofing — perimeter drainage channels installed without excavation, intercepting water before it reaches your floor
  • Exterior Waterproofing with Duraflex Membrane — excavation around the foundation and application of a professional-grade rubberized membrane, stopping water at the source
  • Foundation Crack Repair — polyurethane injection seals cracks permanently and flexibly
  • Weeping Tile Replacement — when perimeter drainage has failed, new drain tile restores proper groundwater management
  • Wet Basement Repair — diagnosis-first approach that identifies the real source before recommending any work
