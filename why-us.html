Why We Chose Duraflex

Duraflex is a rubberized waterproofing membrane that adheres directly to your foundation wall and remains permanently flexible — even through Canada's freeze-thaw cycles. Unlike older tar-based systems that crack and peel over time, Duraflex creates a seamless, high-adhesion barrier that lasts.

✓
Stays flexible through freeze-thaw cycling
✓
Seamless application — no seams to leak
✓
Exceptional adhesion to concrete and block
✓
Withstands high hydrostatic water pressure
✓
Non-toxic, environmentally responsible formula
✓
